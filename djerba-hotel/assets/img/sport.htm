Des sports Albir Playa Hotel & SPA

Nous aimons le sport et nous vous offrons les meilleures installations et services. À Albir Playa Hotel & Sports, nous avons les meilleurs services pour tous les types de groupes sportifs. Nous avons la grande expérience d’accueillir année après année les équipes de football professionnel des meilleures ligues européennes, les équipes de cyclisme professionnelles et semi-professionnelles, l’athlétisme, le handball, le trekking, le tennis, etc.

Nos collaborateurs, notre climat, nos installations, nos services, notre gastronomie et notre attention personnalisée rendront votre séjour sportif inoubliable.

Voulez-vous venir sur la Costa Blanca et profiter d'un camp d'entraînement à vélo? Notre partenaire Elite Cycling a préparé un camp d'entraînement spécial pour nos clients.

  • Football

    Do you want to train in winter at 20º on the best surfaces - both artificial and natural? With us that´s possible! We can organise training and friendly matches for you. We will adapt to the needs of your team. What are you waiting for!

  • Paddle Tennis

    An increasingly popular sport that you can practise on the best tracks in the area in either individual or group classes. Option of renting equipment. Leave it in our hands, you will enjoy it!

  • Cyclisme

    On bike the Costa Blanca is simply amazing. We organise cycling routes to be able to feel the air and sun whilst peddling, see the green and blue landscapes of the Mediterranean...Unforgetable moments. At Albir Playa Hotel & Spa we are ready and prepared for cyclists. See our cycling brochure
    HERE

  • Basketball

    Train in the best sport facilities where teams such as Real Madrid and Valencia Básquet have spent their preseasons. Together with our hotel it makes the perfect combination of relaxation and sports.

  • Trekking

    Spring, Autumn and mild winters are ideal times to enjoy nature and the many benefits it gives you. Wherever you come from, we help you to make the best routes to discover the paradise around you.

  • Tennis

    We have all types of surfaces available in the area so that you can play on your preffered court. And after some training...you can relax at our SENSES SPA.

  • Golf

    The Mediterranean golf courses with an amazing climate and perfect landscapes are unique. Once you try it, you´ll definitely be back! At Albir Playa Hotel & SPA we have deals with the best courses in the area and a team of professionals who will help you manage your booking by advising you on the best course for you.

  • athlétisme

    An atheletes paradise is at Albir Playa Hotel & SPA. Our hotel is equipped with a gym, spa and sports shops. Do you want to see where champions train? Come and discover it!

  • Handball

    Year after year national teams such as Ademar León and international teams from the best leagues in Europe, have chosen us to host the stages prior to the competition. We will work to make your job easier and ensure that everything runs smoothly!
